001 : GlucoMate

"GlucoMate" is a mobile application designed to support individuals managing diabetes in their daily health monitoring. With a user-friendly interface, it allows users to easily track their glucose levels, along with other key health parameters like blood pressure, physical activity, calorie intake, and insulin dosage. The app provides insightful analysis through charts and statistics, aiding both users and healthcare professionals in understanding health trends.

Tools Used :

  • Frontend: Expo, React Native
  • Backend: Firebase

Features :

  • Real-time tracking of glucose levels
  • Monitoring of blood pressure
  • Tracking physical activity
  • Recording calorie intake
  • Management of insulin dosage
  • Insightful analysis through charts and statistics
  • Medication management
  • Data export for collaboration with healthcare providers
